External Direct Product
Let \(G_1, G_2, \ldots, G_n\) be a finite collection of groups. The external direct product of \(G_1, G_2, \ldots, G_n\), written as \(G_1 \oplus G_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus G_n\), is the set of all \(n\)-tuples for which the \(i\) th component is an element of \(G_i\) and the operation is componentwise. In symbols, \[ G_1 \oplus G_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus G_n=\left\{\left(g_1, g_2, \ldots, g_n\right) \mid g_i \in G_i\right\} \]
